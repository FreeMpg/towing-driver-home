⚠️ Do not finalize or modify the original templates.

Steps for Use:
1️⃣ Click the appropriate template button above.
2️⃣ Next, in the upper right corner, by “Charge Customer”, click the three dots (···).
3️⃣ Select “Duplicate Invoice” from the dropdown menu.
4️⃣ Edit the new copy — add battery, towing, or release amounts as needed.
5️⃣ Click “Review Invoice” → “Finalize” and "Continue" to create the payment page.
6️⃣ Present to customer for Tap-to-Pay or enter card information manually.
7️⃣ If Customer requests, return to your newly paid invoice and choose “Send Receipt”.
